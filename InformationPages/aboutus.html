About Us

To lead is to serve. The vision behind E-Kiosk is one of providing service to our community by making their lives as convenient as possible.

We believe transport is a vital part of living your best life. Being able to easily access transport opens you up to more job opportunities, more holiday destinations, more date locations and most importantly more freedom.

With our service we hope to provide all these benefits to all MyCiti users at all income levels.
